Grilled Chicken & Avocado Salad

Nutritional Info

450 kcal

30 g

15 g

Ingredients

6 items

Cooking Guide

1

Prepare the base

Wash and dry the mixed salad greens. Place them in a large bowl and add the halved cherry tomatoes.

2

Slice the ingredients

Slice the grilled chicken breast into 1/2-inch strips. Pit and slice the avocado into thin wedges.

3

Assemble & Dress

Top the greens with chicken and avocado. Drizzle with olive oil dressing and a pinch of salt and pepper.
